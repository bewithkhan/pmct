Allah Helps Those Who Help Themselves

Paisley Muslim Community Trust (PMCT) is situated in the city center, 600 meters from the high street and 250 meters from the University of the West of Scotland (UWS). PMCT serves the community in and around Paisley. The mosque has a capacity of around 200, with a separate ladies' section. It is well equipped with separate male and female wudu areas and toilets.

PMCT is a registered charity (no SC044603) with the following aims:

  • The advancement of education and religion.
  • The promotion of religion or racial harmony.
  • Assistance to the needy.
PMCT provides facilities for regular five-time prayers, Quran teaching five days per week for children, and separate Quran teaching for ladies in beginner and advanced groups.

Our Services

Mosque Development

Our commitment to mosque development ensures that our place of worship remains a vibrant and welcoming space for all, continuously improving to meet the needs of our growing community.

Charity & Donation

Through your generous donations, we support vital community services and provide assistance to those in need, embodying the spirit of charity and compassion in Islam.

Quran Learning

We offer comprehensive Quranic learning programs for all ages, fostering a deep connection with the Holy Quran and promoting a lifelong journey of spiritual growth.

Hadith & Sunnah

Our teachings focus on the Hadith and Sunnah, guiding our community to live by the example of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in every aspect of life.

Parent Education

We empower parents with the knowledge and skills to raise children grounded in Islamic values, ensuring the next generation thrives in faith and character.

Marriage

Our marriage services provide guidance and support to couples, helping them build strong, harmonious relationships rooted in Islamic principles.

Every Muslim Needs To Realise The Importance Of The "Pillars" Of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam form the foundation of a Muslim's life. By embodying these principles, one ensures that their life revolves around faith, prayer, and commitment to the greater good.
